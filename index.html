█ Te Papa Cafe

Opening Hours: 9am–6pm, every day of the year


Our ground-floor cafe offers great breakfasts, brunches, lunches, and fresh snacks. Gluten-free and vegetarian options are available. Drinks include espresso coffee, herbal teas, wine, and beer. Our kids’ play area will entertain your little ones while you rest your feet. A parents’ room is also available.
